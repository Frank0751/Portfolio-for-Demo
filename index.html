About Me

I am a Junior Web Developer at MEST Africa, with expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend tools like Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. I am passionate about leveraging technology to create impactful solutions.

Beyond tech, I founded Sustainability with Koomson (SWK), a non-profit focused on empowering youth and promoting sustainable community development in Ghana. I also serve as a Youth Sounding Board Member at UNDP Ghana, where I advocate for greater youth involvement in decision-making.

My goal is to use my skills in web development and sustainability to drive positive change and inspire the next generation of changemakers.
